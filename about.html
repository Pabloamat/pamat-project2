About Me

Pablo Amat is a New Orleans based artist, songwriter, and keyboardist. His story starts all the way across the Atlantic, in Spain.
He was born and raised in Madrid and started piano lessons at the age of 7. He played the usual “Chopsticks” and simple tunes. His father
is a musician, a big producer in Spain who worked with many people. Music runs in the family.

At the age of 10 however, him and his family had to move to Miami due to the 2008 economic crisis. This was a massive change for the Amat
family. They left their friends, home and family to build a new life in the United States. The first year was rough, it affected all of
them. The new language, people and culture was hard to get used to so Pablo decided to stop playing piano. At the age of 15 he discovered
a new love and appreciation for music. He started listening to big rock artists like Billy Joel and Queen. This re-awakened his love for the
piano and started teaching himself the tunes. Once in Loyola, he realized he would need something more to further his musical abilities, so
he's been studying jazz as a minor. 

He has worked with many local artists like Gabe Parsons, Mynthia and CM Young. Currently, he has started his own project playing his
original music with a killer band. All there is left to say is watch out, because he's going to rock this town!
